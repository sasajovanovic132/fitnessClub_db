Business Rules

Description:

Local Fitness Club is based on Appointments between Fitness Trainer and Client. Fitness Trainer creates Session for customers.They meet via Appointment to execute daily Session.

Subjects:

Fitness   
Client   
Session 
Appointment 

Rules:

-- Fitness Trainer can have Many Clients
-- Client can have Many Fitness Trainers
-- Every Session is created by one Fitness Trainer
-- Every Fitness Trainer can create many Sessions
-- Session is composed of Nutrition Plan ,Work Out plan and Cardio Plan
-- Every Session is paid daily
